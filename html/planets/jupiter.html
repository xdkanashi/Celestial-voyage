Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System, and the fifth one from the Sun. It's mainly composed of gas and is known for its powerful gravitational forces.

Jupiter has distinct cloud bands and the Great Red Spot - a storm in its atmosphere that has been raging for centuries. The planet has over 70 moons, including four large ones named after Galileo, who discovered them in 1610.
